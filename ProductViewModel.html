Class: ProductViewModel

ProductViewModel

Represents a product.

new ProductViewModel(pProduct, params)

The Product View Model is used to handle product details in category selection, product list, search results and product details. A product view model may represent one or more SKUs with varying prices, and which may currently be a sale item.
